Privacy Policy

App: SheriFish  |  Last updated: 7 June 2026

1. Overview

SheriFish ("the app") is a fishing companion app that displays tide times, weather conditions, and fishing information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The app has no user accounts, no registration, and no server-side storage of user data.

3. Location Data

The app may request access to your device's location to display tide times and fishing conditions relevant to your area. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

4. Public Data Fetched by the App

SheriFish retrieves publicly available data from external sources (such as tide databases and weather services) to provide up-to-date information. This is operational data used to power app features — it is not user data and does not involve any personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may fetch data from third-party public APIs. These services operate under their own privacy policies. We do not share any user data with these services.

6. Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely to retrieve tide, weather, and fishing data. No personal information is transmitted over the internet.

7. Children's Privacy

SheriFish does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
